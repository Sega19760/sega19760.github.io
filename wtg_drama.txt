

<h2>The political drama in wtg</h2>
<p>
In defiance of tango, who was the owner of the wtg gc, 3 members in alex, oofer, and paxton made what they called the “active council”. This would spark debate between certain members like trip and cosmik who thought this was stupid. However, it had some corruption behind the scenes.
</p>

<p>
The most major occurrences were action 007 and 003, which banned paxton and cosmik + trip from the group chat permanently. However, a clause in the action gave alex a bribe in a Steam game in both acts, and one act (003) gave paxton a Steam game as well.
</p>

<p>
Other attempts for oofer to ban people happened but didn’t work, including action 012 to ban mellody. This would all come to a halt however with an action coming along to fully ban them, which passed.
</p>


<h2>Action 003 + Action 007, and the lawsuit</h2>
<p>
Action 003, otherwise known as the “oofer and alex friends and gifting agreement”, was an action that would ban 2 members of wtg which included trip and cosmik. However, in the action, alex and paxton were gifted games which caused controversy because it was considered a “bribe”. This action would be reversed later on.
</p>

<p>
Action 007 was an action to ban the wtg member paxton. This had the same issues as action 003, with alex being bribed to say yes in the action. However, paxton would join on an alt because the action didn’t say anything about alts.
</p>

<p>
This would culminate in oofer suing alex and paxton and asking for alex to give up gamemodes, and paxton to be permanently banned from alts. Tango would decide against oofer, due to the fact that the action never said anything about alts. This action would be reversed later by action 008.
</p>


<h2>Leaking incidents in wtg</h2>
<p>
An ongoing issue has been present involving what is considered oofer’s address, as it has been sent multiple times across the server and gc. This has caused other dramas to occur, like action 007, and an attempt to get “revenge” on paxton by oofer.
</p>

<p>
The most serious incident of this leakage occurred in April 2026, where paxton sent oofer’s address, along with his city, zip code, and pictures from the inside of his house. This caused alex to issue a contingency protocol, locking down the old general to admins only.
</p>

<p>
Another incident occurred when oofer leaked tango’s age in a group called the mini hosting community in January 2026, almost causing a permanent removal of oofer. However, this would be reversed hours later by alex.
</p>
